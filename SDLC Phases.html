SDLC Phases Definitions

Concept something conceived in the mind : thought, notion. 2. : an abstract or generic idea generalized from particular instances.
Inception the establishment or starting point of an institution or activity.
Iteration Iteration is the repetition of a process in order to generate a sequence of outcomes. Each repetition of the process is a single iteration, and the outcome of each iteration is then the starting point of the next iteration. In mathematics and computer science, iteration is a standard element of algorithms.
Release to free from confinement, bondage, obligation, pain, etc.; let go.
Maintenance the act of maintaining something or someone: such as. a : the act of keeping property or equipment in good condition by making repairs, correcting problems, etc.
Retirement the action or fact of leaving one's job and ceasing to work.
